Privacy Policy

Privacy at a Glance

  • Your journals and S.O.S. lists are stored locally on your device by default

1. Introduction

At WiseMind, we believe your mental health journey is deeply personal. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our DBT skills training application.

We've designed WiseMind with privacy as a core principle. Your most sensitive data — including your journal entries and S.O.S. lists — stays on your device by default, giving you complete control over your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

Information Stored Locally (On Your Device)

By default, the following data is stored only on your device. We cannot access, view, or retrieve this information unless you explicitly enable cloud synchronization:

  • Journal Entries: All thoughts, reflections, and personal entries you create
  • S.O.S. Lists: Your personalized coping strategies and emergency contact information
  • Progress Data: Your skill practice history and personal notes
  • Custom Settings: Your app preferences and personalization choices

Information We May Collect

When you use certain features of WiseMind, we may collect:

Data Type Purpose Storage
Account Information To create your account (if you choose cloud sync) Our servers
Email Address Account recovery and important updates Our servers
Usage Analytics To improve app performance (anonymized) Analytics provider
Crash Reports To fix bugs and improve stability Error tracking service

3. Cloud Synchronization — Your Choice

We understand that some users want to access their data across multiple devices or keep a secure backup. That's why we offer optional cloud synchronization.

Cloud sync is entirely optional. You can enable or disable it at any time from your Profile Settings.

When cloud sync is enabled, your journals and S.O.S. lists are encrypted and stored securely on our servers, allowing you to access them from any device where you're logged in.

What Happens When You Enable Cloud Sync

  • Your journal entries and S.O.S. lists are encrypted and uploaded to our secure servers
  • You can access your data across all your devices
  • Your data is automatically backed up
  • Data is encrypted both in transit and at rest

What Happens When You Disable Cloud Sync

  • New entries are stored only on your device
  • Previously synced data can be deleted from our servers upon request
  • Local data on your device remains unaffected
  • You can re-enable sync at any time

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the WiseMind application
  • Sync your data across devices (if you enable this feature)
  • Send important service updates and security notifications
  • Improve app performance and fix issues
  • Develop new features based on aggregated, anonymized usage patterns

We never:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Read your journal entries or personal content
  • Use your data for advertising purposes
  • Share identifiable information with researchers or institutions without consent

5. Data Security

We take the security of your information seriously:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted to our servers is encrypted using TLS. Stored data is encrypted at rest using AES-256.
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls limit who can interact with our systems.
  • Regular Audits: We conduct regular security reviews and vulnerability assessments.
  • Secure Infrastructure: Our servers are hosted on reputable cloud providers with enterprise-grade security.

6. Data Retention

Local Data

Data stored on your device remains there until you delete the app or clear the data manually. We have no control over locally stored information.

Cloud-Synced Data

If you use cloud sync, your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can:

  • Delete individual entries at any time
  • Request deletion of all cloud-stored data
  • Export your data before deletion
  • Delete your account entirely

7. Your Rights & Choices

You have control over your data:

  • Access: View all data associated with your account
  • Portability: Export your data in a standard format
  • Correction: Update or correct your account information
  • Deletion: Delete your data or account entirely
  • Consent: Change your cloud sync preferences at any time in Profile Settings

8. Children's Privacy

WiseMind is not intended for children under 13. If you are between 13 and 18, please use the app with parental guidance. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. When we make significant changes, we will notify you through the app or via email (if you've provided one). We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
